Frequently Asked Questions
How do I upload course materials?
Navigate to Materials in the sidebar, then click Upload New Material. Select the course, choose a PDF file, add a description, and click Upload. AI summaries can be generated automatically if the AI toggle is enabled.
How can I track student attendance?
Go to Attendance in the sidebar. Toggle the attendance window open for the relevant course at the start of class. Students can then check in within the open window. You can view present/absent counts in real time.
How do I create a quiz?
Click Snap-Test in the sidebar. A dialog will appear where you can enter a test title, select a course, set the number of questions, duration, and due date. Click Create Test to publish it to your students.
Can I schedule automated announcements?
Currently, announcements are posted immediately when you click Post Announcement. Scheduled/automated announcements are on our roadmap — use the Feature Request category in this support form to vote for it!
